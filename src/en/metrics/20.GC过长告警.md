若Gc时间过长则发出告警，告警策略中的SQL可参考下文：
```SQL
* | select promql_query('sum(rate(go_gc_duration_seconds_sum[1m])) by (instance) /sum(rate(go_gc_duration_seconds_count[1m])) by (instance) > 0.1') from metrics limit 1000
```
